Our Books

Being an 'indie', The Bell Bookshop offers a different and more eclectic range to the chain shops. You'll find books here that the bigger stores ignore and hidden gems you may have overlooked. Everyone working here is passionate about books, and we're always happy to share our latest favourites with you (and we read a lot). We pride ourselves on hand-picking every book on our shelves

We specialise in children's books for all ages, new and general fiction in hardback and paperback, classics, biographies, travel guides, history books, cookery books and other non-fiction and our independent collection.
